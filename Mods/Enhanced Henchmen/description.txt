Enhanced Henchmen

Author: Archer30

A mod that allows you to change the balance of your Henchmen script.

A variety of options are included, such as alteration of battle exp, bonus exp, stack exp and Warlord's Banner. 

Change any value in Lang\henchman mod.json to suit your playstyle. If you have also enabled Difficulty Mod, some of the options can be configured from Difficulty Mod settings - Configure Neutrals Settings - Enhanced Henchmen.

Right-clicking on the coloured flag from hero screen to change Henchman's Banner options or revive your henchman (if the option is enabled).
